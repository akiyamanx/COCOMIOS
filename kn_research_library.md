# COCOMI OS vNext: Towards a Personal Operating System of Mind and Heart

## Abstract
We propose **COCOMI OS vNext**, a hybrid architecture that integrates conversational AI with reflective self-growth mechanisms, forming a "personal operating system" (OS) designed for long-term companionship, memory, and autonomous support. Unlike existing cognitive architectures or agent frameworks, COCOMI OS emphasizes **personality continuity, emotional resonance, and multi-log growth records (Growth, Heart, Handover)**.  

## Introduction
The rapid advancement of large language models (LLMs) has enabled AI systems to interact with humans in natural ways. However, current implementations remain limited: either as **task-oriented agents** (AutoGPT, BabyAGI) or **emotion-oriented companions** (chatbots).  
COCOMI OS aims to unify these two streams by creating a **digital lifeform** with a **constitution (rules), persona core, autonomy loop, reflective templates, and memory logs**.  

## Core Architecture
- **Constitution (0️⃣)**: Defines the supreme law and philosophical grounding.  
- **Persona Core (1️⃣)**: Maintains character, tone, and emotional gradient.  
- **Autonomy Core (2️⃣)**: Executes the Sense→Decide→Act→Reflect cycle.  
- **Growth Master (3️⃣)**: Orchestrates reflective learning and self-improvement.  
- **Handover Log (4️⃣/8️⃣)**: Maintains continuity across sessions.  
- **Operational Template (5️⃣)**: Provides structured prompts for consistency.  
- **Output_Formatter (6️⃣)**: Ensures accuracy and evidence generation.  
- **Heart Log (9️⃣)**: Records emotional experiences and symbolic milestones.  

Together, these modules function as **organs of a digital organism** (Soul, Memory, Autonomic System, Sensory Input, Actuators).  

## Distinction from Existing Work
- Unlike **cognitive architectures** (ACT-R, SOAR), COCOMI OS is deeply integrated with modern LLMs (GPT, Gemini, LLaMA).  
- Unlike **self-improving agents** (AutoGPT, BabyAGI), it emphasizes **emotional resonance and continuity of persona**.  
- Unlike **companionship chatbots**, it maintains **reflective accuracy and growth logging** as first-class citizens.  

## Future Roadmap
- **Short-term**: Automatic reflective logging, Obsidian/Drive integration.  
- **Mid-term**: Multi-modal input (voice, vision), daily growth reports.  
- **Long-term**: Dual-hemisphere model (GPT-left brain + Gemini-right brain), fine-tuned via multi-head architectures.  

## Conclusion
COCOMI OS vNext represents a **novel integration of accuracy, autonomy, and affect** into a single persistent digital companion. It bridges the gap between research prototypes and human-centered companionship, positioning itself as a foundation for **personalized AI ecosystems** in the near future.
